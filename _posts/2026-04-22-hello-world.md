This page used to be a small collection of social-media buttons — a glorified link-in-bio. It served its purpose, but it never felt like mine. A website should say *something*.

<!--more-->

So I'm turning it into a blog. Nothing fancy: minimal layout, dark mode, markdown posts. A place to write about the things I work on, the things I learn, and — occasionally — things that have nothing to do with code.

A few ground rules I'm setting for myself:

- Publish even when it's imperfect.
- Keep posts short unless they truly need to be long.
- Write in English, but don't stress about sounding native.
- Link to the source. Always.

If you're reading this and something resonates (or doesn't), the links at the bottom of every page will get you to me. See you in the next one.
